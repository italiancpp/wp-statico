C++ Day 2018

Two weeks ago, on November 24th, we had an awesome day about C++ in Pavia: the C++ Day 2018. We co-organized this event with the University of Pavia and in particular with two proactive guys: Riccardo Brugo and Federico Pasqua. The event was hosted by the Physics Department and by the director Alberto Rotondi which actively supported the C++ Day. Many thanks to all …
